    Jackie Chan

    Written March 3, 2024
    By Erwin Salamat Jr.

    Jackie Chan, or Chan Kong-san, is an influential Chinese actor, stuntman, martial artist, and filmmaker who is famous for his incredible stunt work, martial arts, and great comedy in his films. He has starred in more than 150 films and has received multiple awards for his amazing success.

    He was born in Hong Kong on April 7, 1954, where he learnt acrobatics, martial arts, and singing at a young age.     After the success of his early films, he would later direct and star in more films, gaining international success in the film industry. He developed a unique style of martial arts and comedy throughout the years in his filmmaking and acting. One of his biggest blockbuster successes was the buddy-cop film Rush Hour (1998), due to its great comedic timing and action. He would gain massive attraction from the American film industry and has become an influential figure to many actors all around the world.

    In 2012, Jackie Chan would announce his retirement from doing martial arts and action films since he was getting old and tired. Ever since his retirement from action films, he has starred in multiple animated movies, which were also huge successes. In addition to this, he also became a UNICEF GoodWill Ambassador and has assisted multiple charities. Jackie Chan has become one of the most legendary Chinese actors and filmmakers in our present time.
